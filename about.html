Who are Ski Dreams?

Ski Dreams is a skiing organisation based within the summits of Adventurous Mountain. It aims to provide the best resources and facilities, and a safe environment for skiers, no matter what level they are, to enjoyable the thrilling experience of skiing.

Since its establishment back in 2015, Ski Dreams has grown and expanded for good recognition from all over the world and has received various awards, like the 'Resort of the Year' award at the 2022 Winter Resort Awards.

Ski Dreams also provides lessons for people of all ages through our Solo, Family and Group packages which you can find on the Book Your Session page of the website. This gives our visitors an exciting opportunity to learn, improve and show off their skiing skills from the help of our friendly and very experienced Skiing Instructors.

If you wish to know more about Ski Dreams or have any questions you'll like to ask, head over to the Contact Us page and we'll be happy to help.
